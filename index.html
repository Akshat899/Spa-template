Our philosophy is rooted in the Japanese concept of ma — the sacred pause between moments — and the Ayurvedic tradition of treating the whole self. We create space for you to simply be.

Our Story

The Soul of
Serenova

Born from a belief that luxury and wellbeing are not opposites, but inseparable companions

Founded 2012

A Vision of
Wholeness

Serenova was founded in 2012 by Dr. Meera Krishnan, an Ayurvedic physician and former luxury hotelier who believed that the world needed spaces where ancient healing wisdom and contemporary luxury could coexist without compromise.

The name Serenova — from the Latin serene and nova (new) — encapsulates our promise: to offer a new kind of serenity, rooted in tradition but relevant to the modern soul.

Today, our team of 24 expert therapists has delivered over 60,000 treatments, each one a unique ritual crafted to the individual in that moment.
